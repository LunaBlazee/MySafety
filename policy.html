Data Protection Rights:

  • you have the right to request access to any copies of your personal data held by MySafety.

Right to rectification:

  • you have the. Right to request the correction of any inaccurate information that is held by MySafety. Furthermore, you can request us to complete information that you believe is incomplete.

  • you have the right to request your personal data to be deleted from my Safety under certain conditions. We will securely erase your data when it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected. (Ideally after exams).

Right to object Data processing:

  • you have the right to object to MySafety’s processing of your personal data under certain conditions. We will respect your objection and assess the necessity of processing your data.

Right to Data portability:

  • You have the right to request the transfer of the data that MySafety has collected about you to another organization or directly to you under certain conditions this allows you to have control over your data.

Exercising your Rights:

Cookie Policy:

  • This cookie policy is going to tell you how we use cookies and similar technologies on our app. By using my Safety, you give full consent to the use of cookies as describe in this policy.

What are cookies:

  • small text files which are placed on your device when you use Our app they help us enhance user experience by remembering your preferences and providing content that suits your needs and therefore analyzing the app performance.

Types of cookies we use:

  • a. Essential cookies: these are necessary for the basic functionality of our app e.g. Cookies that keep you logged in.
  • b. Authentication cookies: these cookies are crucial for user authentication after log in, they ensure that you remain logged in as you navigate through the different sections of the app.
  • c. Identification and authentication cookies: we use cookies to uniquely identify your device and authorize your access to specific features and resources within the mySafety app.
  • d. Password Hashing cookies: For enhanced security, we use cookies to securely hash and store your password, ensuring that sensitive information is protected.
  • e. Analytics cookies: this helps us understand how you interact with the app and enable us to improve features/ overall performance. The information is anonymized and aggregated.
  • f. Functionality cookies: These enhance your experience by remembering your preferences. E.g. frequency use of SOS button.

How we use cookies:

  • We use cookies to make your interactions with the app more efficient and personalized. They help us analyze app usage patterns, identify areas for improvement and enhance overall performance. It will also store your preferences making it easier for you to navigate and access relevant content.

Consent Mechanism:

  • By using the MySafety app you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. We provide a cookie banner during the apps onboarding process which will all you to manage your cookie preferences.

Managing your Cookie preferences:

  • You have the option to control and manage your cookie preferences. This can be done through the app settings where you can choose to accept or reject certain types of cookies.

Third party cookies:

  • My Safety does not use third party cookies for tracking and advertising purposes. All cookies used within the my Safety app are for essential functions related user authentication, identification, authorization, and password hashing.

Duration cookies:

  • Session cookies which are deleted when you close the app and persistent cookies which are stored for a specific duration, enhancing your experience over multiple sessions.

Changes to our Cookie Policy:

  • Any changes to our cookie policy will be communicated through the app. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies. If you have any questions or concerns about cookie policy or data privacy practices please contact us through the apps designated support channels.

Cookie Security:

  • our cookies will be transmitted securely using encryption protocols to protect your data during the transmission process. Cookies are designed to be accessible only by the MySafety app and are not shared with external parties.
